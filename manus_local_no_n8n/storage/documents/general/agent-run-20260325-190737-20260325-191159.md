Here are 5 creative things you could do with your kids' art:

## 1. **Family Memory Book/Scrapbook**
- Compile artwork from different ages into a scrapbook
- Add captions, dates, and stories about each piece
- Perfect for preserving milestones and developmental progress

## 2. **Wall Gallery Display**
- Frame pieces in a gallery-style arrangement
- Create themed sections (e.g., "Doodles," "Landscapes," "Portraits")
- Rotate displays seasonally or by occasion

## 3. **Gift Wrapping & Tags**
- Use artwork to decorate gift boxes
- Create personalized gift tags with their drawings
- Great for birthdays, holidays, or special occasions

## 4. **Time Capsule Project**
- Save artwork in dated envelopes
- Store in a box with notes about what was happening in your life
- Open together in a year or two for nostalgia

## 5. **Artwork-Based Merchandise**
- Print favorite pieces for t-shirts, mugs, or tote bags
- Create custom pillows or blankets with their art
- Make postcards or greeting cards to send to family/friends
